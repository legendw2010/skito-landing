Every Toronto Kid Deserves the Mountain

Toronto has a skiing community - it's just never had a home. SkiTO is built to change that.
Whether you're eager to ski but don't know where to start, looking for your child's first
lesson as a parent, wanting to reach more kids as a coach, SkiTO brings it all together in
one place. We believe every Toronto kid deserves access to the mountain, regardless of their
background or budget. Through partnerships, resources, and a connected community, SkiTO is
turning that belief into something real.Toronto's ski community has always needed.

Everything You Need,
All In One Place

From local ski hills to subsidized programs and gear guides, SkiTO makes it easy to find exactly what you're looking for. Search by location, age, skill level, and budget, and get on the mountain faster!

You're Not Skiing Alone

SkiTO connects you with programs, resources, and fellow skiers across Toronto. Share experiences, discover events, and become part of a network that's passionate about getting more kids on the slopes!

The Slopes Are For Everyone

No budget? No problem. SkiTO connects underprivileged youth with subsidized programs, donated gear, and partner hills that believe every Toronto kid deserves a shot at the mountain!

Built for Toronto. Built for Everyone.

SkiTO brings together all the tools, resources, and connections that
Toronto's ski community has been missing — in one place, for everyone.

  • Resource Finder

    Search hills, programs, and lessons near Toronto. Filter by cost, age group, and skill level to find the perfect fit for any young skier in the city.

  • Youth Access Portal

    Browse free and subsidized ski programs built specifically for youth who need a hand getting started. SkiTO makes sure cost is never the reason a kid misses out.

  • Hill Partner Network

    Partner hills post openings and availability directly on SkiTO, making it easy to find and book programs. A direct line between Toronto youth and the slopes they deserve.

  • Gear Exchange

    Donate used ski gear within the Toronto community. Good equipment shouldn't go to waste, and every kid deserves to show up to the hill ready to ride.

  • Learning Resources

    Beginner guides, safety tips, and technique videos all in one place. SkiTO's learning hub has everything you need to get started with confience.

  • Event Board

    Stay on top of fundraisers and community events happening across Toronto. One board, every event, all season long.
